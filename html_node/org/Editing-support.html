12.8.5 Editing support

The org-beamer-mode is a special minor mode for faster editing of Beamer documents. 

     #+STARTUP: beamer
C-c C-b     (org-beamer-select-environment)
The org-beamer-mode provides this key for quicker selections in Beamer normal environments, and for selecting the BEAMER_COL property.
